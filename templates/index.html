Encryption and Decryption

Encryption locks your message into a secret form only the right key can open. Decryption unlocks it back to readable text. Together, they protect your data from curious eyes while sharing information safely, simply, and confidently online every single day.

User Guide

1 Encryption

  1. Click on 'Encryption' to start.
  2. Enter your secret message or upload a .txt file.
  3. Create a unique 'Key' (password).
  4. Hit 'Encrypt' to get your scrambled code.

Example

Input: "Hello"

Key: "Secret"

Result: "X7#m9P..."

2 Decryption

  1. Navigate to the 'Decryption' page.
  2. Paste the encrypted code or upload the file.
  3. Enter the SAME 'Key' used for locking.
  4. Click 'Decrypt' to reveal the original text.

Example

Input: "X7#m9P..."

Key: "Secret"

Result: "Hello"

Note: Keep the KEY safe and dont loose it otherwise your data will never be decrypted
